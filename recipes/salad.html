Salad Recipe

Description

A salad is a versatile dish of mixed ingredients, usually raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, grains, or proteins, tossed with a dressing and served cold or warm, ranging from simple leafy greens to complex pasta, bean, or fruit mixes

Salad is, literally and figuratively, what you make it. Really, the only parameter is that a hodgepodge of (usually cold, but not always) ingredients be unified by a dressing

Ingredients

  • Vegetables (carrots, onions, cucumber etc..)
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Spices
  • Seeds and Nuts

Steps

  1. Wash and Dry Produce
  2. Chop items into Bite-Sized Pieces
  3. Combine all items
  4. Add Virgin Olive Oil
  5. Add your Seasoning (salt, black pepper) or Spices
  6. Taste test
  7. Dress the Salad
  8. Toss the Mixture Gently
  9. Serve Immediately
