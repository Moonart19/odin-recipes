Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is a popular dish featuring a cooked, ground meat (usually beef) patty, or a veggie/alternative patty, served inside a sliced bun or roll, often with toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, bacon, and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, or mayo.

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables (carrots, onions, cucumber etc..)
  • Bun (Sliced)
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Grill the Patty
  2. Pan Fry with oil and add patties
  3. Oven Bake the seasoned patties
  4. Toast Your Buns
  5. Melt the Cheese
  6. Rest the Meat
  7. Assemble Strategically and Enjoy
